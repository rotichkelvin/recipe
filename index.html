Chocolate Chip Cake Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 cup flour
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup chocolate chips

Instructions

  • Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour a 9x9 inch pan.
  • In a large bowl, combine flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  • In another bowl, mix milk, oil, and vanilla. Add to dry ingredients and stir until just combined.
  • Stir in chocolate chips. Pour batter into prepared pan.
  • Bake for 25-30 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  • Cool in pan on wire rack. Cut into squares and enjoy!
